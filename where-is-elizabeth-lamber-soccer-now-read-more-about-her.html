Elizabeth Lamber is a soccer player. She was a New Mexico women’s soccer defender.

Even though there’s no information about her early life, family, parents on the internet, there’s a lot to learn from the controversial soccer player.

Her Educational Background

She attended the University of New Mexico, where she led the Lobos to their first-ever NCAA championship game. Lambert is an aggressive player, but her skills and athleticism have made her one of the most respected female players in the world.

Who Is Elizabeth Lambert’s Husband?

How Her Football Career Started

Elizabeth Lambert was the first female soccer player to play for the New Mexico Lobos. Her domination of the game, and for not holding back is what gained her love from her admirers.

Her no-holds-barred playing style made her a force to be reckoned with, but also earned her a reputation for being tough and aggressive.

Her legacy lives on in the New Mexico Lobos soccer program, which she helped launch, and in the university’s annual Elizabeth Lambert soccer tournament.

Her tackles and clashes with other players caused some to call her a “dirty player.” But Lambert didn’t care.

She was embraced her hard-hitting image and even went on to say “If you’re not pissing people off, you’re not doing something right.”

Lambert’s fearlessness and determination on the field changed the game of soccer by paving the way for future women athletes who were afraid to be aggressive.

She proved that it was possible to be a successful woman athlete and play the game the way you want to play it—no matter what society thinks.
