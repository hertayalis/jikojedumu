How to Get Neon Cat in Pet Simulator X

There are two ways to get this new pet. The first method is to trade for it. The second is to purchase an Exclusive Pets Egg. To hatch one of these Eggs, you will need to pay 400 Robux for it. Three such Exclusive Pets Eggs can be bought for 1200 Robux, and ten of them for 3200 Robux. As we have already mentioned, there are several pets that you can get from this Exclusive Egg 8, and the odds of getting them are as follows: Neon Cat (50% chance, valued at 600,000,000 Diamonds), Neon Dog (35% chance, valued at 700,000,000 Diamonds), Neon Griffin (13% chance, valued at 1,100,000,000 diamonds), Huge Neon Cat (1.25% chance, valued at 800,000,000,000 Diamonds), Huge Neon Griffin (0.75% chance, valued at 1,200,000,000,000 Diamonds), and Titanic Neon Agony (0.05% chance, valued at 100,000,000,000,000 Diamonds).

We wish you the best of luck and we hope that you are going to be able to get many of these Neon Pets in Pet Simulator X.
