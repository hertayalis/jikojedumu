Megan Fox Gets In on Euphoria Trend While Showing Off Engagement Ring

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Even Megan Fox can't resist dressing up for Euphoria High.

Since the release of the second season of HBO's Euphoria, TikTokers have meme'd out the characters' eclectic and edgy senses of style—specifically by showing off the eclectic and edgy outfits they'd wear to the show's fictional East Highland High School, affectionately dubbed Euphoria High on the app. Think fishnets, lace, cutouts, and lots and lots of sheer.

Yesterday, Fox threw her hat in the ring, sharing new photos of herself dressed as a teacher at the infamous school. (Our first reaction: This is the perfect opportunity to get a Jennifer's Body crossover in the works — somebody get Sam Levinson on the phone).

The actress wore a matching Dion Lee set in slate gray featuring a sleeveless tank top with a wide chest cutout and straight-leg trousers with an asymmetrical belted waist. She opted for Amina Muaddi accessories, including a dusty-blue patent-leather mini bag with a crystal-embellished strap and lilac pumps.

"If I were the AP Literature teacher at Euphoria High," she captioned the photos.

Fox's glam was also extremely Euphoria-esque, with bright-orange eye shadow matching her neon manicure. A few selfies also showed off Fox's emerald-and-diamond engagement ring, given to her by Machine Gun Kelly, who proposed to the star earlier this month.

Of the ring's design, MGK told Vogue …

It’s a thoroughbred Colombian emerald, with no treatment. It was just carved into the teardrop, straight out of the mine. And the diamond was directly from [jeweler Stephen Webster]. The concept is that the ring can come apart to make two rings. When it’s together, it’s held in place by a magnet. So you see how it snaps together? And then it forms an obscure heart. And you see this right here? The bands are actually thorns. So if she tries to take it off, it hurts. ... Love is pain!
