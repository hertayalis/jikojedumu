Sanditon on MASTERPIECE: Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

If you have already been swept away by Sanditon on MASTERPIECE on PBS, or if you are watching for the first time, we’ve compiled answers to the most common fan questions about the period drama series.

  • 1.

    How many episodes are there of Sanditon?

    Season 1 aired eight episodes from January 12-February 23, 2020 on MASTERPIECE on PBS.

    Season 2 aired six episodes from March 20-April 24, 2022 on MASTERPIECE on PBS.

    Season 3 aired the show’s final six episodes from March 19-April 23, 2023 on MASTERPIECE on PBS.

  • 2.

    Where can I watch Sanditon?

    Full episodes of Sanditon Seasons 1, 2 and 3 are available on PBS Passport, an added member benefit. With PBS Passport, you get extended access to a digital, on-demand library of more than 1,500 episodes of PBS favorites, including select MASTERPIECE shows.

    You can watch Sanditon on the PBS MASTERPIECE Prime Video Channel (available to Amazon Prime members for an additional monthly cost). You can also see Sanditon via Apple, Comcast, Google Play, FandangoNOW, Verizon and Vudu. The series is not available on Netflix.

    DVDs and Blu-rays of Sanditon are available from ShopPBS.org, where every purchase you make supports public broadcasting. As a Sanditon fan, we’re pleased to offer you an exclusive offer to purchase Sanditon or anything else in the MASTERPIECE Collection at ShopPBS.org.

  • 3.

    How Many Seasons of Sanditon Are There?

    Sanditon‘s third season is its last. “We’re sad to take leave of this marvelous series, which has all the wit and joy expected of a Jane Austen-inspired drama,” says Susanne Simpson, Executive Producer of MASTERPIECE. “We know viewers will be on the edge of their seats as the final pieces fall into place.”

  • 4.

    Where was Sanditon filmed?

    While Sanditon isn’t a real-life location, you can find out more about the beaches, stately homes, and historic locations featured in the series in our official Sanditon locations guide.

  • 5.

    Why didn’t Jane Austen finish Sanditon?
