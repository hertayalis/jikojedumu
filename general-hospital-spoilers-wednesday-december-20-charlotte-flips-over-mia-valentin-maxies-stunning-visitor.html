General Hospital Spoilers: Wednesday, December 20 Charlotte Flips Over MIA Valentin Maxie

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for Wednesday, December 20, reveal that Portia Robinson (Brook Kerr) will put Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Alexander Chavez) on the spot when she asks what he really knows about love.

Spencer may admit he doesn’t have a lot of experience with it, but he knows what he feels with Trina Robinson (Tabyana Ali) is the real deal.

It turns out Spencer will make some progress with Portia and get invited to stay for some fun holiday merriment.

Over with Marshall Ashford (Robert Gossett) and Stella Henry (Vernee Watson), they’ll have an impromptu dance party and strengthen their bond in the process.

As for Hamilton Finn (Michael Easton), he’ll be uncertain of what to do as he opens up to Elizabeth Baldwin (Rebecca Herbst).

Finn may have all sorts of concerns about the malpractice lawsuit and how it could derail his whole career if he loses this case.

Next, Violet Finn (Jophielle Love) will tell Jake Webber (Hudson West) that her daddy’s going away, so she may worry Finn will end up doing a stint in prison like Scout Cain’s (Cosette Abinanate) dad, Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison).

Jake may assure Violet that Finn isn’t going anywhere and won’t be giving up his daddy role – even if he loses his case and his job at GH.

General Hospital spoilers actually claim Violet will have a heartfelt conversation with Aiden Webber (Enzo De Angelis), but it looks like Jake will be filling in – perhaps due to actor availability.

Speaking of actor availability, Courtney Fulk will continue her temporary run as Josslyn Jacks in Wednesday’s episode.

The preview video shows Josslyn asking Adam Wright (Joshua Benard) what he’s still doing there, so she may be surprised to find him at the dorm instead of home for Christmas.

Adam might admit his holiday plans fell through – or maybe he’ll make up some flimsy excuse about meeting up with some people later. Either way, Adam will once again find himself drawn to Josslyn.

Dex Heller (Evan Hofer) will witness some troubling warning signs on Wednesday and express concern. It may seem like Adam is tangling Josslyn up in his problems and becoming way too dependent on her.

Meanwhile, Damian Spinelli (Bradford Anderson) will show up on Maxie Jones’ (Kirsten Storms) doorstep all wet, so she’ll be shocked when he says he requires her urgent assistance.

As for Charlotte Cassadine (Scarlett Fernandez), she’ll fume over Valentin Cassadine (James Patrick Stuart) going MIA on Christmas Eve and wonder what he could possibly be doing.

After Charlotte gets all worked up and complains to her grandmother, Laura Collins (Genie Francis) and Kevin Collins (Jon Lindstrom) will worry about how she’s holding up.

Elsewhere, Anna Devane (Finola Hughes) will meet with Valentin to offer updates on John Brennan’s (Charles Mesure) arrest.

Valentin and Anna will presumably discuss Brennan’s role as the WSB director as well as his Pikeman connection.

Now that this nightmare is hopefully behind them, Valentin might wonder if there’s hope for a future with Anna after all.

However, a tearful Anna will insist she can no longer be part of Valentin’s family after everything that went down with Charlotte.

General Hospital spoilers suggest Anna will stand her ground when it comes to the end of their relationship, so that’ll bring tough news for Valentin.
