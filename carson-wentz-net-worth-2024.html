Carson Wentz’s Football Career

Carson Wentz started his professional football career with the Philadelphia Eagles in 2016. His exceptional performance and leadership skills quickly made him one of the most valuable players in the team. His move to the Colts also came with a substantial salary, contributing significantly to his net worth.

Major Endorsement Deals
