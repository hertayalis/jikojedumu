Kristen Stewart Continues Her Menswear Style Streak at Chanels Cruise Show

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Chanel darling Kristen Stewart showed up to support the French fashion house at its stunning Cruise 2023–2024 runway show in Los Angeles yesterday evening.

She joined fellow Chanel ambassadors Marion Cotillard, Elle Fanning, and Margot Robbie, as well as other celebrities and friends of the house at the event hosted at Paramount Studios.

For the grand occasion, Stewart wore a gray tweed Chanel suit with a subtle checkered pattern. The set consisted of a short blazer and straight tailored pants. Ever the minimalist, the actor styled it simply with a classic white tee, glossy black shoes, and a big silver chain necklace with the brand’s “CC” logo. She wore minimal, glowy makeup and embraced the messy wet look with her short hair.

Variety//Getty Images

Stewart also wore a menswear-inspired look by Chanel to this year’s Met Gala. There, she opted for a black-and-white suit made up of a cropped white blazer with a black collar, tailored black pants, a nude-and-white collared top made to look like a mini vest, and a short black tie, which doubled as a bow, with the Chanel logo. She finished the cool look with white-and-black shoes by the French fashion house and a black belt with the “CC” logo in gold inside a buckle featuring the house’s iconic gold metal-and-black leather chain motif.

The Spencer star also wore Chanel looks to the 2022 and 2019 Met Galas. Last year, she walked the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s carpeted steps in a cropped tuxedo with hot shorts, paired with a semi-sheer white button-up and black pumps. And at the 2019 event, Stewart showed out in a punk-rock look consisting of a sequined black top, loose white pants, black-and-white platforms, and layers of Chanel accessories, including a belt and beaded necklaces.
